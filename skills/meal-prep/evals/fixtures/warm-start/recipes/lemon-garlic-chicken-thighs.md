# Lemon-Garlic Chicken Thighs

One-line description: Crispy-skinned sheet-pan chicken thighs with a bright
lemon-garlic pan sauce — easy weeknight win.

## Ingredients
- 2 lb bone-in chicken thighs
- 4 clove garlic
- 2 lemon
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 lb baby potatoes
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp black pepper

## Steps
1. Heat oven to 425°F. Toss potatoes with 1 tbsp oil, salt, pepper on a sheet pan.
2. Rub chicken with remaining oil, minced garlic, oregano, zest of 1 lemon.
3. Nestle chicken among potatoes; roast 30–35 min until 175°F internal.
4. Squeeze juice of both lemons over everything; rest 5 min and serve.

## Prep & storage
- Make-ahead: marinate chicken up to 24 h.
- Storage: fridge 3 days.
- Reheat: 375°F oven 10 min to keep skin crisp.

## Notes
- Swap potatoes for green beans in the last 15 min for a lighter version.
