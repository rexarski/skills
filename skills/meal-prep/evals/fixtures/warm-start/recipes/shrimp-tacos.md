# Shrimp Tacos

One-line description: Quick spiced shrimp with cabbage slaw and lime crema in warm
tortillas — 25 minutes start to finish.

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb shrimp
- 8 corn tortilla
- 3 cup shredded cabbage
- 1 lime
- 0.5 cup sour cream
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 0.5 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Toss shrimp with paprika, salt, oil. Sauté 3–4 min until pink.
2. Mix sour cream with lime juice for crema.
3. Warm tortillas; build with shrimp, cabbage, crema.

## Prep & storage
- Make-ahead: slaw and crema up to 1 day.
- Storage: best fresh; shrimp fridge 2 days.
- Reheat: quick skillet, don't overcook.

## Notes
- Keep mild for the kids; add hot sauce to taste.
