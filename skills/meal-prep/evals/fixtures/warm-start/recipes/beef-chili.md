# Beef Chili

One-line description: Hearty slow-simmered beef and bean chili that freezes
beautifully — a batch-cooking staple.

## Ingredients
- 2 lb ground beef
- 2 onion
- 3 clove garlic
- 2 can kidney beans
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Brown beef in oil; remove. Soften diced onion and garlic.
2. Return beef; add spices, tomatoes, drained beans.
3. Simmer 35–40 min until thick. Salt to taste.

## Prep & storage
- Make-ahead: tastes better next day.
- Storage: fridge 4 days; freezes 3 months.
- Reheat: stovetop with a splash of water.

## Notes
- Mild for the kids — serve hot sauce on the side for adults.
